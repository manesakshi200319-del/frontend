JEON JUNGKOOK

Jeon Jungkook is a South Korean singer and member of BTS. He debuted in 2013 and is known as the group's main vocalist. Jungkook is famous for his singing, dancing, stage presence, and songs like Seven and Standing Next to You.

JEON JUNGKOOK

Jungkook was born on September 1, 1997. Fans often call him the "Golden Maknae" because of his talents in singing, dancing, sports, and performance.

JEON JUNGKOOK

His solo music gained global popularity and reached international charts. Jungkook continues to inspire millions of fans around the world.
